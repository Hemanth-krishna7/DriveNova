# Motion Guidelines

Motion should communicate depth.

Motion should never exist purely for decoration.

Scrolling should feel cinematic.

Mouse movement should create subtle perspective.

Everything should feel physically believable.

---

## Preferred Motion Characteristics

- smooth
- slow
- premium
- restrained

### Avoid
- bounce
- elastic motion
- exaggerated scaling
- flashy transitions
- playful interactions

---

## Preferred Duration Ranges

### Fast
200–300ms

### Normal
350–500ms

### Slow
600–900ms

---

## Motion Hierarchy

1. Background moves least.
2. Lighting moves subtly.
3. Vehicle moves moderately.
4. Typography moves slightly more.
5. Glass overlays move the most.
6. Navigation moves minimally.

*Foreground should always move more than the background.*
